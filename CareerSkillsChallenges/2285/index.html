Find Your Forever Friend


Here at Central Valley Pet Adoption, we believe pets can come in any size. We are a welcoming shelter dedicated to finding loving homes for both traditional and exotic pets. Located in the heart of the community, our center offers a safe haven for a wide variety of animals, from playful dogs and cats to fascinating birds and snakes. With a team of passionate staff and volunteers, Central Valley Pet Adoption provides exceptional care for every animal, ensuring they are healthy, socialized, and ready for their new families. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse selection of pets, catering to families and individuals looking for a traditional companion or those seeking something a bit more unique. Whether you're looking for a cuddly kitten, a loyal dog, or an exotic bird to add to your home, Central Valley Pet Adoption is the place to visit, where every pet deserves a second chance at a loving life.
